Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time with immediate access to new features. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my staff limit?

You'll need to upgrade to a higher plan or remove inactive users to add new team members.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes, we offer a 15% discount for annual billing. Contact our sales team for more information.

How do I create custom learning paths?

Custom learning paths are available on Standard and Premium plans. You can create them from the Learning Paths section of your admin dashboard.

Show me the difference between an implant and a bridge.

9:35 AM

ðŸŒ¿

Here's a concise, patient-friendly explanation: A dental implant is a titanium post surgically placed into the jawbone that acts as an artificial tooth root. A crown is attached to this post, creating a standalone replacement tooth. A dental bridge, on the other hand, uses the teeth on either side of the gap as anchors to support a false tooth in the middle. Implants don't affect adjacent teeth and better preserve bone health, while bridges are less invasive initially but may require replacing those anchor teeth with crowns.
